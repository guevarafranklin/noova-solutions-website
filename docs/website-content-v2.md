### Our Story

**Headline:**
How we got here.

**Body:**
Noova Solutions started with a simple observation: US companies need great tech talent, and Latin America has it in abundance. The gap was never about skill — it was about access, trust, and structure. We set out to bridge that gap.

We began by connecting experienced developers and engineers with US teams that needed to grow fast but didn't want to spend months hiring. We learned quickly that timezone alignment, cultural compatibility, and rigorous vetting made the difference between a contractor who checks boxes and a team member who truly contributes.

As we grew, we noticed something else. Small businesses and entrepreneurs across the Americas were drowning in social media messages. They were losing real sales — not because their products were bad, but because they simply couldn't respond fast enough. A customer would ask about a product on Instagram at 10 PM and by 8 AM the next day, they'd already bought from someone else.

That frustration became Comerciafy — an AI agent that handles customer conversations and sales on social media, 24/7, in Spanish and English. It was born from a real problem we watched real businesses struggle with, and it's now helping entrepreneurs across the Americas sell more without hiring more.

Today, Noova Solutions operates at the intersection of talent and technology. We help companies build their engineering teams and we build AI tools that help businesses grow. Both sides of our company share the same mission: give businesses the resources they need to move faster.
