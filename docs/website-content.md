### The Problem

**Headline:**
You're losing sales in your DMs right now.

**Body:**
If you sell through social media, you know the pain. Messages pile up. Customers ask the same questions over and over. You can't respond at 2 AM. By the time you reply, the buyer already went to your competitor. Hiring a full-time salesperson is expensive. Training them takes time. And they still can't work 24/7.

---

### The Solution

**Headline:**
Comerciafy handles it for you.

**Feature 1 — Instant Responses, 24/7**
Comerciafy replies to customer messages in seconds, any time of day. It answers product questions, shares prices, and provides information — even while you sleep. No more lost leads because you took too long to respond.

**Feature 2 — Intelligent Lead Qualification**
Not every message is a sale. Comerciafy identifies serious buyers by asking the right follow-up questions, understanding purchase intent, and prioritizing high-value conversations for your attention.

**Feature 3 — Multichannel, One Dashboard**
Manage WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and Facebook Messenger from a single place. Comerciafy keeps all conversations organized so nothing falls through the cracks.

**Feature 4 — Bilingual by Default**
Comerciafy communicates fluently in both Spanish and English, switching naturally based on the customer's language. Perfect for businesses serving the US Hispanic market or operating across Latin America.

**Feature 5 — Learns Your Business**
Comerciafy isn't a generic chatbot. You train it with your product catalog, pricing, FAQs, and brand voice. It sounds like you, not like a robot.

**Feature 6 — Human Handoff**
When a conversation needs a personal touch — a complex negotiation, a complaint, a VIP customer — Comerciafy seamlessly hands the chat to you or your team with full context.

---

### How It Works

**Headline:**
Up and running in 3 simple steps.

**Step 1 — Connect Your Channels**
Link your WhatsApp Business, Instagram, and Facebook accounts. It takes minutes, not hours.

**Step 2 — Train Your Agent**
Upload your product catalog, set your prices, add your FAQs, and define your brand tone. Comerciafy learns how your business talks and sells.

**Step 3 — Start Selling**
Comerciafy goes live and starts handling customer conversations immediately. Monitor results from your dashboard and step in whenever you want.

### Our Story

**Body:**
Noova Solutions was founded with a straightforward idea: US companies need great tech talent, and Latin America has it in abundance. The gap isn't skill — it's access. We bridge that gap.

We started as a staff augmentation company, connecting experienced developers and engineers with US teams that needed to scale fast. We saw firsthand how timezone alignment, cultural proximity, and rigorous vetting made nearshore talent not just a budget alternative — but a genuine competitive advantage.

As we worked with more clients, we noticed another pattern: small businesses and entrepreneurs across the Americas were struggling to keep up with sales conversations on social media. They were losing customers simply because they couldn't respond fast enough. That's why we built Comerciafy — an AI agent that handles customer interactions and sales so business owners can focus on what they do best.

Today, Noova Solutions operates at the intersection of talent and technology. We help companies grow their teams and sell smarter.
